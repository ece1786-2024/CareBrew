About CareBrew

CareBrew is an interactive AI-powered training module for customer service workers in coffeeshops. This project was created by Dasha, Dylan, James, and Joe for ECE1786: Creative Applications of Natural Language Processing.

Training

Training mode helps you learn empathetic responses to coffee shop-related scenarios through real-life cases and using empathetic review.
